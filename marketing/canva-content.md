### Slide 4: Process
**Section:** Process
**Title:** How It Works
**Subtitle:** A collaborative, transparent process from first conversation to final reveal.

**Step 1 — Consultation:** We discuss your vision, space, timeline, and goals. Whether it's brand identity, community message, or personal expression, we start by listening.

**Step 2 — Concept:** Based on our conversation, I develop initial concepts that capture your vision while bringing creative expertise to the design.

**Step 3 — Design:** The chosen concept is refined into a detailed design mockup. You'll see exactly how the mural will look on your wall before any paint is applied.

**Step 4 — Painting:** Using professional-grade materials and techniques, the mural comes to life. OSHA-certified for safe operation on any scale.

**Step 5 — Reveal:** Your completed mural is unveiled. High-resolution photos document the finished work for your use in marketing and social media.
